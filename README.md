# Morgan Stanley – Enterprise Financial Intelligence Suite

## Project Overview
This project simulates an **enterprise-grade Business Intelligence solution** for a global investment bank like **Morgan Stanley**, built using **Power BI** and a large-scale **synthetic financial dataset**.

The objective is to demonstrate how executive leadership, wealth managers, investment bankers, risk teams, and operations teams can use data-driven dashboards to monitor **financial performance, client profitability, risk exposure, advisor productivity, and compliance indicators**.

The solution is designed as a **10-dashboard analytics suite**, similar to real-world BI platforms used in tier-1 financial institutions.

---

## Key Business Areas Covered
- Executive financial performance & growth
- Wealth management AUM and client segmentation
- Investment banking revenue & deal pipeline
- Trading performance and market risk
- Client profitability and lifecycle analysis
- Advisor and desk productivity
- Portfolio allocation insights
- Client experience and engagement
- Compliance, AML, and operational risk
- Client-level drill-through analytics

---

## Dataset
- **File:** `morgan_stanley_synthetic_financials.csv`
- **Records:** 100,000 (synthetic)
- **Grain:** Client–Account–Month
- **Time Period:** 2021–2024

The dataset is fully synthetic and does **not** contain any real client or financial data. It is intended strictly for **analytics, visualization, and portfolio demonstration purposes**.
